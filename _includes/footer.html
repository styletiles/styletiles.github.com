Open source your design process

Style Tiles work for me. Designers all have different challenges. I am sharing my process to contribute back a solution to a problem that I have encountered. Open Source is all about sharing what works for us and building upon that to help progress our industry. Share what works best for you, and help elevate the quality and standards of our chosen professions.

To learn more about the origins of my process check out this blog post at badassideas.com
