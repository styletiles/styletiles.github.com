Who

Style tiles are for people who design websites and website design systems.

What

What is a Style Tile?

A design deliverable consisting of fonts, colors and interface elements that communicates the evolution of a visual brand for the web.

What Are They Good For?

Style tiles help to form a common visual language between the designers and the stakeholders and provide a catalyst for discussions around the preferences and goals of the client.

How to Explain Style Tiles to Clients, Friends & Other Interested Parties

A style tile is a tray of interface design snippets that define words that the stakeholders agree represent their brand. It is similar to the paint chips and fabric swatches an interior designer gets approval on when designing a room.

When

Style tiles are for when a moodboard is too vague and a comp is too literal. Style tiles establish a direct connection with actual interface elements without defining layout. They work well for clients who have established brands and need them to translate smoothly to the web.
